What to Expect?

Training classes

Capoeira is an Afro-Brazilian martial art that combines elements of acrobatics, music, and self-defense. You'll learn fundamental movements like the ginga (the basic step), kicks, dodges, and acrobatics. Classes combine physical training with music, rhythm, and cultural education in a supportive community environment.

The Roda

The practice involves two players interacting in a "roda" (circle) while responding to music played by the surrounding participants. The music consists of instruments like the berimbau (a musical bow), pandeiro (tambourine), and atabaque (drum) all of which you will learn over time.

Cultural Heritage

Beyond its physical aspects, Capoeira represents a cultural preservation of Afro-Brazilian heritage and offers benefits including flexibility, endurance, power, and resilience. Expect a welcoming, inclusive environment where students of all levels train together. You'll develop flexibility, strength, coordination, and confidence while learning about Afro-Brazilian culture.
